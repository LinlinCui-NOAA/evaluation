• The site displays verifications of global NWP forecasts against 1) surface observations of T2m, RH2m amd wind at 10m over the Continental United Stats and the Alaska region, and 2) upper-air observations of T, RH and Wind from rawinsonde, dropsonde, and profilers over the global and its subregions.
